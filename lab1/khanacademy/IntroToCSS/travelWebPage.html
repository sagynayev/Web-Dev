My Trip to Kyrgyzstan

This year, I went to Kyrgyzstan. It is a beautiful country! My first stop was Bishkek. I saw the big mosque called Ala-Too Mosque. It is very pretty!


Also, I visited Issyk-Kul Lake. The lake is very big and the water is clear. I enjoyed swimming and relaxing on the beach. I finished my trip in Osh, where I saw the famous Suleiman Mountain. The view from the top was amazing!

Kyrgyzstan is a country that is very exciting to explore!
